About FaceReveal

FaceReveal is a web-based personality analysis platform that uses facial detection and predefined behavioral logic to generate personality insights.

The system analyzes facial presence and generates personality-style results using structured logic. These results are intended for entertainment purposes only.

We do not perform real psychological analysis or store facial data permanently.

FaceReveal is designed to deliver a fast, engaging, and privacy-focused experience.
